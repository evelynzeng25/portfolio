Impact

As the Project Manager for Michigan Advertising & Marketing, I led a team of 3 market researchers and 2 creative designers to design and execute a marketing campaign for Blue Front, a historic Ann Arbor liquor store known for its local charm and wide selection.

I worked closely with Blue Front’s management to understand their business needs and identify key marketing opportunities. Through detailed market research and customer surveys, my team gained insights into the preferences of their target audience, which informed a social media strategy designed to increase foot traffic and highlight Blue Front’s diverse product offerings.

My team created engaging promotional content, including graphics and messaging that showcased Blue Front’s unique history and inventory. By leveraging social platforms and a cohesive branding approach, we strengthened Blue Front’s local visibility and helped drive sales during a critical growth period. Regular client check-ins, clear task delegation, and creative collaboration ensured the campaign was both timely and effective, cementing Blue Front’s reputation as a community staple.
